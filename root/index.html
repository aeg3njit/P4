Things to Remember:

"Anxiety happens when you think you have to figure out everything all at once. Breathe. You're strong. You got this. Take it day by day."

Karen Salmansohn

"Worrying doesn't empty tomorrow of its sorrows; it empties today of its strengths"

Corrie Ten Boom

"If you are depressed, you are living in the past. If you are anxious, you are living in the future. If you are at peace, you are living in the present."

Lao Tzu

"Courage doesn't always roar. Sometimes courage is the quiet voice at the end of the day saying, 'I will try again tomorrow.'"

Mary Anne Radmacher

About:

Hello, my name is Alisha Gomes, and I am the creator of this site. I am currently studying Computer Science at the New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT), and I am passionate about helping people struggling with anxiety-related disorders.

Being someone who experiences anxiety, I know how hard it can be to do simple tasks. However, anxiety shouldn't be thought of an impairment, instead it should be thought of as something that can be managed.

I hope this site makes anxiety less daunting than we make it out to be. With playful and inciteful drawings and writing, I hope you can make anxiety your friend.
